Buddist Vihara Murals
Sri Wijaya Nandanaramaya
Egodawaththa , Piliyandala
In the vihara of temple, murals have been created on ceiling and on walls. Additionally, entrance to the inner chamber of vihara also was built newly. Outside is decorated by embossing historical incidents.
Chethiyaramaya
Bandaragama Road , Kidelpitiya
Murals have been drawn in renovated Vihara building. Ceiling is decorated with flower patterns & entrance to the inner chamber was newly built including guarding figures and lion figures.
Suvishuddharamaya
Wellawatta
In the vihara of temple, murals have been created on walls & ceiling is decorated with patterns. In addition there are buddha statues and fully embossed incidents releted to life of Lord Buddha.
Siri Niketharamaya
Nampamunuwa, Piliyandala
In the outer chamber of vihara , murals have been created on ceiling and on walls. Additionally, entrance to the old inner chamber of vihara also was newly decorated with a Makara Thorana.
Sudharmalankara Viharaya
Makuludoova , Piliyandala
In this temple there is a roof around the bodhi-ghara to provide shelter to devotees. Murals depicting six heavens and other importand incidents are drawn on the ceiling of that roof .
Sri Dhammakiththikaramaya
Kottawa , Pannipitiya
In the vihara of temple, murals have been created on ceiling and on walls with brownish tone. Also columns are decorated with picturesque patterns to enhance the grace.
Sath Budu Maha Viharaya
Pelanwaththa , Pannipitiya
In the vihara of temple, background mural is drawn. Further murals have been created in the dome of stupa depicting heavens and in shrine of relics depicting historic incidents.
Saddharmaramaya
Kahapola, Piliyandala
In the vihara of temple, murals have been created on ceiling and on walls. This is one out of latest work done by artist . Murals are in colourful style with brilliant hues.
Sri Dharmendraramaya
Mt.Lavinia
In the vihara building , murals are drawn in a modern style with bright colours. Simplicity is prominent feature in this modern art. However work was completed recently
Sri Dhammindaramaya
Kothalawalapura , Rathmalana
In the temple, stupa has an accessible dome . In the dome of stupa murals have been created in colourful style to depict Lord Buddha's life incidents & other important topics.
